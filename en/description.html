Main features of Foobnix Player

Supports CUE (also wv, iso.wv), this is the best under Linux (zavlab)

Multiple formats available, including MP3, MP4, AAC, CD Audio, WMA, Vorbis, FLAC, WavPack, WAV, AIFF, Musepack, Speex, AU, SND and more

Foobnix supports cross-format exporting for MP3, OGG, MP2, AC3, M4A and WAV

Scrobbler tags with music and radio available

Find and play music and videos

Equalizer

Online music download manager

Shortcuts

Foobnix is able to display the album cover, lyrics, and artist photo

VKontakte integration (displays all the friends and their music, able to download music from VKontakte groups)

Last.FM integration (shows and plays the best songs, favorite songs, and artists)
